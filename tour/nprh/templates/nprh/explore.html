POPULAR PLACES


LADAKH

Ladakh is one of the three major regions in the state of Jammu and Kashmir.Ladakh was divided into two districts in 1979: Kargil and Leh.The Kargil district has a predominant Muslim (76.87%) population.The Leh district has 66.40% Buddhist population.The principal language of Ladakh is Ladakhi.Ladakhi has no distinctive script, writers use Tibetan script to write the spoken language.Ice hockey is the most popular sport in Ladakh.Ice hockey in Ladakh is played from mid-December to mid-February, only on natural ice.Cricket, Archery, and Polo are some other popular sports in the region.The Indus River is the backbone of the Ladakh region.


KERALA

Kerala is one of the most beautiful places in the world. Its sobriquet of God's Own Country is well deserved. The entire expanse of Kerala is blessed with beauty, unmissable attractionsKerala is famous for its unique geography, tranquil backwaters, unspoiled beaches, art forms and spices. It is also famous for its charming houseboats, sprawling tea plantations, unique eco-tourism, magnificent architecture, Ayurvedic treatments and unforgettable culinary experiences.Munnar is considered one of the most beautiful tourist places in Kerala. Situated on the Western Ghats, Munnar is evidently one of the highest producers of tea on the Indian subcontinent.


MANALI

A gift of the Himalayas to the world, Manali is a beautiful township nestled in the picturesque Beas River valley. It is a rustic enclave known for its cool climate and snow-capped mountains, offering respite to tourists escaping scorching heat of the plains.It is a natural divide between the valleys of Kullu and mountains of Lahaul and spiti. It attracts tourist with beautiful views and fresh snow. Here you can enjoy adventure activities such as skiing, skating, paragliding, snow scooter and more. Best time to visit Rohtang pass is in Summer.

RISHIKESH

Rishikesh is primarily famous for its spiritual importance. There are a number of Yoga Ashramas for practicing meditation. Rishikesh is the beginning of the famous Char Dham Yatra (Gangotri, Yamunotri, Kedarnath, Badrinath).It is known as the pilgrimage town and regarded as one of the holiest places for Hindus. Hindu sages and saints have visited Rishikesh since ancient times to meditate in search of higher knowledge. Rishikesh is the world famous yoga center. RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan is renowned for its imposing olden forts and gorgeous palaces, people generally tend to overlook these beautiful Jain temples at Ranakpur, which are quite well-hidden in a dark forest on the banks of the Maghai River.One of the most colourful states in India, Rajasthan is blessed with landscapes of thick forests and sandy deserts, majestic man made creations, flourishing flora and fauna and a history that no other culture can match.
